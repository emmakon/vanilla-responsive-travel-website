Why Wellington?

Wellington, a city of personality

Wellington, New Zealand’s capital, offers a stunning blend of natural beauty and city life. Surrounded by hills and a sparkling harbor, the city’s scenery is a highlight, with spots like Mount Victoria Lookout providing sweeping views. You can explore the waterfront, visit Te Papa Museum, or take the Wellington Cable Car to the Botanic Garden, all within a compact, walkable area.
